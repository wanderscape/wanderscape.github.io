Siargao Island

Why Siargao Should Be Your Next Destination

It is not crowded... yet 

Since Siargao hasn't been discovered yet by lots of people, it is not yet crowded. So the best time to visit Siargao is right now! You can peacefully enjoy the beautiful white sand of the beach!

Plenty of beaches

Siargao Island is an island of island. There are plenty of beaches that you can enjoy with fascinating white sand in it. Also, high quality hotel and restaurants are just within the vicinity, so you can relax all day! 

Surf Capital of the Philippines

You can passionately surf with Siargao's crystal clear water for whatever time you want! It is super cool and all the instructors are very friendly to their guests. surf enthusiasts are provided with a three-level viewing deck that can ensure their safety.

Jungle Disco

Even if the nightlife in this Island is not that vibrant, you can still enjoy the night at the Jungle Disco hidden between the palm trees! You can enjoy their famous drinks, such as their jungle juice!

Sugba Lagoon

How to get there

Rent a tricycle from General Luna to Dapa, where the port is located, which will cost you around P700 and can fit 5-6 people in the tricycle one way. From the terminal port, you can get another trycicle for around P300 to Del Carmen where you can take the boats to Sugba Lagoon in Siargao Island.

What to expect

Sugba Lagoon isn’t as overcrowded as spots like Kayangan Lake in Coron. You won’t have the lagoon to yourselves by any means but it’s a big enough area to find your own piece of paradise to chill, take photos and hang out.

Naked Island

How to get there

You can get to the island by availing an island hopping tour. You can rent a boat for 1500 PHP for maximum of 6 people. The island hopping in Siargao Island includes Naked Island, Daku Island and Guyam. The tours are flexible and they will give you 4-5 hours as much as you need until the sunset time.

What to expect

Naked Island is the place to go to for some sun, sand and swimming.
Get a tan on the island; this is unavoidable because there are no trees or any place for shade during your stay. Swim in its cool waters to douse the heat and relax and laze on the soft white sand.

Daku Island

How to get there

You can get to the island by availing an island hopping tour. You can rent a boat for 1500 PHP for maximum of 6 people. The island hopping in Siargao Island includes Naked Island, Daku Island and Guyam. The tours are flexible and they will give you 4-5 hours as much as you need until the sunset time.

What to expect

The beach is wide enough for activities such as beach volleyball and frisbee. You can also chill and relax in one of the hammocks there.
Take plenty of pictures of the beautiful and idyllic beachscape with its cerulean waters, dancing coconut trees, and bone-white sand.
