Boracay Island

Boracay Station 2

The restaurant, shopping, and entertainment heart of White Beach

All-in-one Area

Station 2 has the best resorts where you can easily and quickly find the perfect place for your stay. Variety of good restaurants can also be found here; D'Tailpapa is a seafood market in this station where you can have your fresh seafood meal. D'mall, the biggest of the 3 shopping areas in Boracay, sits right in the middle of Station 2.

Social Interaction

Walking along Station 2 will never get boring. Since this station has the best resorts and restaurants, lots of tourists of different nationalities are here. You can sit at the shore and unwind while watching tourists walk along the beach.

Your Boracay trip will never get dull. Wanderscape assures you that because Boracay has all the exciting activities to offer: parasailing, scuba diving, helmet diving, name it!

Your stay at Boracay won't be complete without having a taste of Boracay's nightlife. Walk along Station 2 where you can immediately find the best place to unwind and have the best night of your life!

Experience Boracay Submarine

Witness the amazing underwater world and its marine sanctuaries with Boracay submarine. Be captivated in the crystal blue oceans of Boracay as you tour down to 25 meters (82 feet) deep. Explore the aquatic life forms beneath the rushing waves as you sit back and relax in your submarine tour. Dive into the marvelous world underwater without soaking yourself or carrying a tank behind.

La Carmela de Boracay

  • BUDGET-FRIENDLY - Premiere deluxe room costs P3,259.36 per room per night, and can accomodate three to four people. Breakfast is served each day of the stay.
